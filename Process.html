How the process works

Demat issues often remain unresolved due to incorrect escalation or lack of clarity on process ownership. Our approach focuses on understanding the issue first.

Step 1 – Issue details

You share details and documents related to your demat issue.

Step 2 – Process review

The issue is reviewed against standard Depository Participant procedures.

Step 3 – Guidance

You receive guidance on the correct next step and expected timelines.
