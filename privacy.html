Privacy Policy (Summary)

We collect only the information you provide in the complaint form to file and track your grievance. We do not sell your personal data. We share only what is necessary with the relevant regulatory authorities and DPs for the purpose of complaint redressal.

Data retention

We retain complaint data only as long as necessary to resolve the complaint and comply with legal requirements. Contact dematshield@outlook.in for data deletion requests.

Security

We follow privacy-first handling and recommend removing irrelevant personal details from any screenshots before uploading.
