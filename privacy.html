Privacy Policy

Demat Shield respects the privacy of investors and is committed to protecting any information shared with us.

We collect only the information necessary to understand the demat account issue raised by the investor. This may include copies of communication, transaction statements, or basic account details.

We do not ask for passwords, PINs, OTPs, or trading credentials. Investors should never share such information with anyone.

Information shared with Demat Shield is used solely for providing guidance and is not sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties.
