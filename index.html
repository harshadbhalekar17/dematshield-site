What Demat Shield helps with

Guidance is limited to demat account operations that fall under the responsibility of the Depository Participant.

Demat account closure & AMC issues

Delays, rejections, or charges continuing after closure requests.

Demat, remat & transfer delays

Issues in dematerialisation, rematerialisation, or security transfers.

Incorrect demat records

Name, bank, nominee, or status mismatches causing operational blocks.

Unclear debit or credit entries

Unexpected entries where explanations are incomplete or unclear.

Important boundaries

No access to your demat account

We never ask for OTPs, passwords, or login credentials.

Guidance only

We do not act on behalf of investors or file complaints.

No guaranteed outcomes

Guidance helps you decide the next step. Outcomes depend on the DP.
