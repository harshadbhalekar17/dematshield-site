About Demat Shield

Demat Shield was created to simplify grievance filing for retail investors. We guide you through the correct process to file complaints with DPs, brokers, and regulatory portals like SEBI SCORES.

Our mission

To make investor grievance redressal accessible, fast and secure — with clear templates and active follow-up.

What we do

  • Help craft and lodge complaints to relevant authorities
  • Provide templates & escalation steps
  • Protect privacy and handle documents responsibly
