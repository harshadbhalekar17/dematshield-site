About Demat Shield

Demat Shield is an independent investor guidance platform focused on demat account operational issues.

Many investors face delays or unclear responses from their Depository Participant. Our role is to help investors understand whether an issue falls under demat processes and what the next practical step should be.

We do not act on behalf of investors and we do not guarantee outcomes. Our service is limited to guidance and process understanding.
