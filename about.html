About Demat Shield

Demat Shield is an independent investor guidance platform focused on demat account–related operational issues.

Many investors face delays, repeated follow-ups, or unclear responses when dealing with their Depository Participant. In most cases, the issue is not about wrongdoing, but about process gaps, missing documentation, or misunderstanding of responsibilities.

Demat Shield exists to help investors understand whether their issue falls under demat operations, what the applicable process is, and what the next practical step should be.

What we do

We review the details shared by the investor and provide guidance based on standard Depository Participant processes and documentation.

Our guidance is intended to help investors take informed and appropriate action on their own.

What we do not do

Demat Shield does not act on behalf of investors, file complaints, escalate matters, or represent investors before any authority.

We do not guarantee outcomes or timelines. Final resolution depends on the Depository Participant and the facts of each case.
